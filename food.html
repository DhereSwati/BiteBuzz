Pasta

A staple food of traditional Italian cuisine, made from wheat flour and water. made with love and fresh ingredients. From spaghetti to fettuccine, experience the rich flavors of Italy.

Sushi

A Japanese dish consisting of vinegared rice accompanied by various ingredients, such as seafood and vegetables.

Tacos

A traditional Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various mixtures.

Pizza

A popular Italian dish consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients.
